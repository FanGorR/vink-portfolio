02 — Profile

Currently finishing my degree in Creative Industry Management (Extended Reality) at Universiti Utara Malaysia — Dean's List for five consecutive semesters. Internship placement upcoming.

Alongside filmmaking, I developed VinK Film PowerGrade Vol.1 — a complete cinematic colour grading system for DaVinci Resolve, researched and built entirely from native nodes. Built because I needed it. Released because others did too.

I work across disciplines: directing, cinematography, colour grading, Unity XR development, 3D modelling in Blender, UI/UX in Figma, and data visualisation in Power BI. The common thread isn't the tool — it's the intent to make something that means something.
