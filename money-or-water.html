Dreamer

A quiet film about ambition, doubt, and what it means to try anyway — made alone, in ten days.

Overview

Desmond is an introverted young man with a passion for filmmaking but limited resources and constant external doubt. Encouraged by his friend Kong and supported by his sister, he embarks on his first film project — discovering along the way that the act of trying carries more meaning than the outcome.

Dreamer is about the specific quiet courage it takes to create when you're not sure you can — and completing the work anyway.

The entire production — script, shoot, edit, colour grade — was completed solo from first draft to final cut in ten days. Selected for LOKA International Film Festival Open Category alongside industry and open-public entrants.
