Overview

Song Li is a young man who dares to enter a photography competition despite knowing almost nothing about the craft. The film is a quiet meditation on effort, courage, and the intrinsic value of simply trying — regardless of outcome.

Entered into one of Malaysia's most competitive short film contests and reached the global top 20 out of 70,000+ entries across 180 countries.
