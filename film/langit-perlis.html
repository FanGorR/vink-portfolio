Overview

A travel short documentary showcasing the natural beauty and hidden attractions of Perlis, Malaysia's smallest state. Shot across 7 iconic locations over two days with a two-person crew, the film blends landscape cinematography, voice narration, and a full original colour grade to present Perlis as rarely seen on screen.

The challenge: capture seven distinct locations with cinematic quality in under 48 hours — full colour grade, narration recording, and music arrangement completed from scratch in DaVinci Resolve.
