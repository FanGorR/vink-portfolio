Overview

A full suite of digital assets across seven different media disciplines, all unified under a single cultural theme — Selangor, Malaysia. Each asset demanded a completely different skill set and toolchain — effectively seven mini-projects in one semester.

All assets were published publicly on Behance, Pixabay, and Polycam.

The 7 Assets

  1. ① Graphic Postcard — Selangor landmarks in batik-motif tile style. Adobe Illustrator. Behance.
  2. ② Graphic Illustration — Chinese Kenduri family banquet in papercut style. Adobe Illustrator. Behance.
  3. ③ Custom Font — KONGFont — Full Latin typeface, 潦草铅笔风格. Hand-drawn, exported as TTF. Behance.
  4. ④ 3D Character — Little Cute Mosque — Blue Mosque-inspired mascot. Blender, UV-unwrapped, STL print-ready. Polycam.
  5. ⑤ Photography Series — 3 images: family portrait, lunar astrophotography, golden hour campus. Lightroom. Behance.
  6. ⑥ Ambient Audio — 17 Tracks — Selangor soundscapes (Batu Caves, Port Klang, etc.). 44.1kHz loopable WAV. Pixabay.
  7. ⑦ Narrative Video — 60s cinematic journey across Selangor. AI imagery, ElevenLabs narration, DaVinci Resolve edit. Pixabay.
