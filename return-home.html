Overview

A road safety short film produced for the national competition organised by the Kedah branch of JPJ (Jabatan Pengangkutan Jalan). This was the first production to involve a dedicated crew structure — coordinating an assistant director and a screenwriter through pre-production planning, and managing a larger cast across multiple locations.

The film reached 1.7K views and 204 likes on YouTube, and was awarded 2nd Place at the national level.
