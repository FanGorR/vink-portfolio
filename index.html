01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Vincent Kong, a filmmaker and creative technologist based in Malaysia. I work at the intersection of storytelling and technology , from cinematic short films to XR experiences.

In ten days, alone, I wrote, shot, directed, and edited Dreamer — a film that went on to win 2nd place at LOKA International Film Festival. That's how I work: with urgency, with intention, with everything I have.

Currently finishing my final semester in Creative Industry Management at Universiti Utara Malaysia, Dean's List every semesters.
